6. Learning Mode 1: Episode-Centric

Prep → Watch → Revise

Prep: Before an episode, the user practices key words they are unlikely to know. This reduces friction and maximizes enjoyment.

Revision: After an episode, the user reviews key learning areas, cementing their understanding with exercises from the scenes they just watched.

7. Learning Mode 2: User-Centric

Mastering the language of your favorite shows.

A purely independent experience that takes the user from the easiest (most-frequent) to the hardest (least-frequent) words, phrases, and grammar.
